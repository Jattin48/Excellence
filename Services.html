Common Questions

Everything you need to know about starting your journey with us.

What is the enrollment process? expand_more
The process begins with a preliminary assessment of the student's current academic level, followed by a consultation with parents to define goals. Once a program is selected, registration can be completed online or at our center.
Are sessions available online or in-person? expand_more
We offer a hybrid model. Students can choose between physical classroom environments at our premium coaching centers or high-fidelity interactive online sessions conducted via our proprietary learning platform.
How do schedules work for busy students? expand_more
Our scheduling is highly flexible. We offer early morning, late evening, and weekend batches to accommodate school hours and extracurricular commitments. One-on-one sessions can be custom-scheduled.
Can I access study notes without enrolling? expand_more
While our core resources are reserved for enrolled students, we offer a "Resource Pass" subscription for independent learners who wish to access our digital library and quiz banks without attending live sessions.
